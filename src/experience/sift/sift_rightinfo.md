## Job Description

- Part of two research DARPA projects
- One is MARGARET which is relating to using word embedding to discover and measure gender bias in texts from different regions.
- Published two research papers about Margaret as shown [here](https://www.aclweb.org/anthology/W19-3803/) and [here](http://sbp-brims.org/2019/proceedings/papers/working_papers/Friedman.pdf)
- Worked with many types of word embeddings models including Word2Vec, GloVe, ELMo, and BERT. The next phase of the project was then to try to draw causal inferences between economic or social statistics and gender bias using Bayesian type models.
- Second project was detect hostile intent online that could lead to acts of violence. Mostly done by web scraping toxic internet forums (e.g. incels) and performing NLP models like sentiment analysis and sentence classification.
